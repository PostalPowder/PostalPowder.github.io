Dessert:

Ice Cream Pancake

18 Jan 2017

Ingredients:
1/2 Cup of All-Purpose Flour
1.5 Teaspoons of Baking Powder
1 Teaspoon of Salt
1 Tbs of White Sugar
1/4 Cup of Milk
1 Egg
3 Tbs of Melted Butter
Ice Cream of Choice
Toppings of Choice
Maple Syrup

Prep Time: 5 Minutes

Cooking Time: 15 Minutes

Steps:
1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth
2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot
3. Add your ice cream of choice and sprinkle your toppings over the finished product, also do not forget to drizzle some maple syrup over the pancake or even dip it in a cup of maple syrup
