Breakfast:

Oats Porridge

4 Jan 2017

Ingredients:
1/4 Cup of Rolled Oats
1/2 Cup of Almond Milk (Milk of Choice)
1 Tsp Honey
1/3 of a Ripe Banana
1 Large Strawberry
4-5 Almonds
1 Tbs Peanut Butter
2-3 Strawberries
2-3 Figs
1 Whole Banana
10-12 Almonds
1 Tsp Chia Seeds

Prep Time: 5 Minutes

Cooking Time: 20 Minutes

Steps:
1. Heat Milk till it boils and add oats into the milk
2. Heat under medium heat till Oats are cooked till preferred done-ness
3. Mix Honey into the cooked oats
4. Cut up fruits and garnish
5. Add Almonds and Chia Seeds for a boost of energy
